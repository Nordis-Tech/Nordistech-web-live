Capabilities

Capabilities

Nordis delivers capabilities across defence and civil applications — powered by a common integrated platform. Each capability can be engaged independently or as part of an end-to-end development and fielding pipeline. Built to align with Canada's dual-use defence industrial strategy and NATO interoperability requirements.

01
Simulation & Evaluation

High-fidelity simulation environment for validating system performance before hardware exists. Enables rapid iteration across vehicle, sensor, and autonomy configurations with full scenario control.

HLA IEEE 1516 DIS IEEE 1278 STANAG 4586 C2SIM
Defence Platform validation, threat modelling, doctrine development
Civil Fleet performance testing, environmental scenario modelling
02
Operations Research & Mission Analysis

Define the problem before building the solution. Model mission requirements, fleet sizing, coverage optimization, and performance trade spaces to inform what gets built and why.

Defence Force structure analysis, mission envelope definition
Civil Search and rescue fleet sizing, forestry survey coverage optimization
03
Mission Planning & Deployment

Intuitive tools for translating operational objectives into executable missions. Supports collaborative autonomy in GPS-denied and contested environments — deployable across defence and civil operations.

Defence Autonomous patrol coordination, contested environment navigation
Civil Disaster response deployment, under-canopy mapping, post-event survey
04
Design Optimization

Hardware and software co-design across the full vehicle development cycle. Rigorous optimization methods — from classical optimal control to learned approaches — applied to find the best solution within real operational constraints.

Defence Mission-specific platform optimization, GNC algorithm development
Civil Endurance and payload optimization for survey and logistics applications
05
Warfighter & Operator Training

The same simulation environment that informs design supports operational training — creating a continuous feedback loop between capability development and real-world use. Applicable to both military and civil operator training programs.

Defence Warfighter training, mission rehearsal, doctrine validation
Civil Emergency response operator certification, fleet coordination training
06
Autonomous Vehicle Production

The integrated platform is the foundation for Nordis to design, prototype, and produce its own autonomous vehicles — purpose-built for the missions the platform defines. From first simulation to fielded hardware.

Defence Sovereign Canadian autonomous platforms for allied procurement
Civil Purpose-built vehicles for public safety, environmental, and logistics applications

Each capability stands alone. Together they close the loop from concept to operational deployment — compressing development timelines and reducing the cost of fielding capable autonomous systems.

Independent Product Line
Vocis
Secure Language Intelligence

In high-stakes environments, the accuracy and security of spoken information matters as much as the decision it informs.

Vocis is a private, on-device platform for voice transcription, summarization, and structured documentation — with no data leaving the local environment. Designed initially for clinical and medical applications where transcript integrity and patient privacy are non-negotiable, the platform is domain-agnostic and applicable wherever sensitive voice intelligence requires secure handling.

About Nordis

Nordis is a Canadian technology company building the integrated software platform for autonomous systems development — spanning defence, public safety, and environmental applications — and the tools to deploy them.

Founded by a military veteran and defence engineer who spent a decade inside the primes — now closing the gap between simulation, design, and deployment.

We are based in Vancouver, British Columbia, and built from the ground up to serve Canada and its allied partners.
